The Call Masking Problem

A Growing Threat to Nigerian Telecommunications

What is Call Masking?

CLI Spoofing (Call Masking) is a fraud technique where attackers manipulate Caller ID to display fake phone numbers, deceiving victims into believing calls are from trusted sources.

Common Attack Scenarios:

  • Bank impersonation to steal credentials
  • Government agency fraud
  • Business email compromise calls
  • Targeted harassment campaigns
